About Us

Zero Limit Productions is a freelance video production studio based in Sandy, Utah, founded by filmmaker Drew McKay. What began as a passion discovered in school has quickly evolved into a full-service production business.

From weddings and mission homecomings to commercials, stage productions, and real estate, Zero Limit Productions delivers high-quality, compelling video tailored to each client.

From filming on a smartphone to now operating with professional gear—including the Lumix S5 II, DJI Mini 4 Pro drone, and more—Drew has rapidly expanded both his skillset and his equipment. As Zero Limit Productions continues to grow, so does the commitment to delivering cinematic, high-quality video for any occasion

Thank you for visiting our site — let’s make something unforgettable.
